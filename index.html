About ShieldNet

A modern IT partner for every device, network, and office

ShieldNet Technologies is a modern IT services brand focused on delivering professional, affordable, and reliable technology support for individuals, small businesses, offices, and institutions across Sri Lanka.

From setting up a home Wi-Fi network to maintaining the full IT infrastructure of a growing office, our technicians bring the same standard of care — transparent pricing, tested solutions, and support that doesn't disappear after the job is done.

We combine hands-on repair expertise with a security-first mindset, so the systems we build and fix stay protected long after we leave.
